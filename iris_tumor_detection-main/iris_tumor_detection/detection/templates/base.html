Iris Tumor Information

Iris tumors are abnormal growths located on the iris of the eye.
These tumors can be benign or malignant. Early detection and monitoring are essential for effective treatment and care.
Using CNNs for iris tumor detection aims to enhance diagnostic accuracy, support early intervention, and streamline the analysis of iris images, ultimately contributing to more effective patient care.
